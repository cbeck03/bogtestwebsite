About
Welcome to Beasts of Galdrjoro, an open world survival game where you the player get the explore the landscape while surviving as one of many mythological beasts or humanoids. Takes flight in the skies as a fearsome dragon, explore the ocean depths as the majestic water horse, or hop through the fields and dense forests as the adorable Jackalope.

There is only one goal in this world: survive. It 's a kill or be killed world, with every encounter more dangerous than the last. While you aren 't fighting for your life or stalking prey, take part in the procreation of life to sustain and ensure your species survival. Or maybe you 're a solo player who takes a liking to exploration and adventure. With over 100 square kilometers of land, ocean, mountains, and kingdoms, you 're sure to keep busy.

This game is more than just surviving as your favorite beast from mythology. It 's about the journey to adulthood and elder beyond, along with creating new memories with friends. Supporting a large capacity of players, join this world in our official multiplayer servers with your friends to truly enjoy the survival experience in Beasts of Galdrjoro.
